Solutions & Conclusions

Right now, people all over the world are trying to stop deep sea mining before it causes serious harm. Some countries like France, Canada, and several Pacific Island nations want to pause or ban deep sea mining until we know more about the damage it could cause.

Organizations like Greenpeace are working to protect ocean life by spreading awareness and asking companies not to use deep sea minerals.

Lara’s Ideas!

  • Before mining, invest in ocean research to learn more before we cause harm.
  • Recycle materials from old electronics to reduce the need for mining.
